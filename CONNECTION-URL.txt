# PostgreSQL Connection URL for BasketJoin

## Full Connection URL (Copy & Paste This)

postgresql://basketjoin:basketjoin_dev_password@localhost:5432/basketjoin_dev

## URL Components Breakdown

Protocol:  postgresql://
Username:  basketjoin
Password:  basketjoin_dev_password
Host:      localhost
Port:      5432
Database:  basketjoin_dev
